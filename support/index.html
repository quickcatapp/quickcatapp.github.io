QuickCat Support

Help, contact, and how to report issues.

Contact us

The fastest way to reach us is by email. We read every message.

Email: quickcatapp@gmail.com

When reporting an issue, please include: device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to play?

No. QuickCat has no sign-up, no login, and no profile. Your nickname, round history, themed level progress, and daily-game status are stored on your device only.

Why was my answer marked invalid?

Answers are validated by an AI judge against the category and starting letter. If you think an answer should have counted, tap the report icon next to the answer on the results screen. Reports are reviewed and used to improve validation.

I lost my progress after reinstalling.

Because QuickCat stores progress locally with no account, uninstalling the app removes that data. We do not currently support cross-device sync. If this is important to you, let us know — we're tracking demand.

My daily streak reset unexpectedly.

The daily streak counts consecutive days with a completed daily game. Skipping a day, changing your device's date/time, or uninstalling will reset it. If you believe there's a bug, please email us with the steps.

The app is asking for tracking permission. Why?

iOS shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt the first time the app runs. We use this identifier only to deliver more relevant ads through our ad networks. You can decline and continue playing — gameplay is unaffected. You can also change this later in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

How do I report an inappropriate ad?

Open the in-app Settings screen and tap Report an ad, or email us at quickcatapp@gmail.com with a screenshot. Include the time the ad appeared if possible — this helps our ad networks identify the creative.

How do I disable ads?

QuickCat is free and ad-supported. We do not currently offer an ad-free option. Ads are limited (no ads during active rounds, at most one interstitial every 90 seconds, and rewarded ads are always optional).

Does QuickCat work offline?

Answer validation requires an internet connection because answers are checked by an AI service. The rest of the app (browsing levels, viewing history, starting a round) works without a connection, but you'll need to be online to see results.

Privacy and data

QuickCat does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information. Anonymous gameplay data and answer reports are stored on our servers to operate the game and improve validation. Ad networks collect data per their own policies.
